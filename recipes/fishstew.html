Fish Stew

Description

This is a recipe for making delicious fish stew. You can make them at home.

This is an easy recipe to execute. You don't need to be a culinary expert, OK?

Ingredients

    Fish
    water
    seasonings
    carrots

Steps

    Cook the fish.
    Boil the broth.
    Add the fish to the broth.
    Let simmer for 10 hours.
