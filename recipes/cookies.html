Cookies

Description

This is a recipe for making delicious cookies. You can make them at home.

This is an easy recipe to execute. You don't need to be a culinary expert, OK?

Ingredients

    flour
    sugar
    eggs
    chocolate chips

Steps

    Mix the flour, eggs, and sugar together.
    Add chocolate chips..
    Put on baking sheet in small balls 4 inches apart.
    Bake for 8 minutes or until golden brown.
