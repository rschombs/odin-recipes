Chicken Fingers

Description

This is a recipe for making delicious chicken fingers. You can make them at home.

This is an easy recipe to execute. You don't need to be a culinary expert, OK?

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • breadcrumbs
  • eggs
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken by slicing it.
  2. Make the egg wash and drench the chicken.
  3. Cover it with breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry for 3 minutes or until golden brown.
